About

About Me

Hello! My name is Delilia Werner. Ok, full disclosure. My full name is Ruby Delilia Baker Werner. I am named after both my grandmothers- Ruby Bell Langston Brackner and Bertha Delilia Emfinger Baker. As I thought of a name for my business, I was reminded of the strong family ties to both sides of my family- beginning with my name. I wanted my company to reflect those ties, as family-yours, mine, and theirs- is what it is all about. My passion for finding those that are lost in time knows no familial bounds!

I have over 20 years experience researching my own family. I started doing my own family history research earnestly in 2003 while dragging my youngest son to cemeteries and the homes of newfound family connections. I have also helped many friends and a few clients. I later pursued a more formal path of learning and graduated with an associate degree in Family History Research from BYU-Idaho in 2022. I continue learning by taking courses offered online through various websites with a goal of becoming an Accredited Genealogist though ICAPGen.
I have research experience mostly in the Southeast United States, but I have done client work in other states such as New York and Missouri, as well. . Using a solid research plan and good methodology allows any place to be researched. I have many genealogy subscriptions that grant me access to many different record types.

Fees

Document Look-up Service

All document look-up fees are based on the following:

Internet Search-5.00 for under fifteen minutes, and an additional 5.00 for every fifteen minutes added. Includes source citation and research log.

Location searches- .75c per mile within 100 miles.
Repositories near me:
Georgia Archives 87 miles.
NARA Southeast Region 87 miles.
FamilySearch Macon, Ga. center 20 miles.
Washington Memorial Library Macon, Ga. 23 miles.

Added to the cost whether internet or in-person:

Digital copy-free
Photocopy- .50c per document + actual mailing cost (to include envelope and postage) Original record-cost of record + actual mailing cost (to include envelope and postage)

Research Plan

Included: Consult to discuss your genealogy goal, analyze any records for reliability, and develop a plan for you to follow.
$40 per hour, with a minimum of 5 hours
$200 for a 5-hour research plan
$400 for a 10 hour research plan

Report with Research Log and Copies of Documents

Included: Consult to discuss your genealogy goal, analyze any records for reliability, research documents that will most likely help you reach your goal, write a report with the findings.

$800 for a 20-hour project
